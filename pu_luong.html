Pu Luong Natural Reserve

1 day Tour
Previous Next × Close
Early in the morning we will open our eyes for a beautiful day and eat some refreshing tropical fruits. Our journey towards Pu Luong National Park will start at 7:30 by a van or motorbikes. We will start climbing the magnificent nature reserve and stop for an authentic Vietnamese breakfast in front of ancient terraces' landscape. As we ride, we can see traditional houses of ethnic people who live at the foothills of the mountains among their farms. We will stop at a number of amazing observation points to overlook the deep green valleys of the reserve, and you will be able to stop at points on your request. We will get down into the valley of Phố Đoàn and Cổ lũng villages to experience the local markets. On the way to the villages we will pass by the regional river that the villagers use for agriculture,. We will see the rice fields that are flooded by an authentic method with beautiful water wheel stations. After all of the this goodness, we will get off from the transport and make a short walk towards the clear river of bright blue colours. We will step into the shallow water and go through stunning steps of Hiêu waterfall. A little immersion in running water is perfectly refreshing. Now it is time to have a Vietnamese lunch in one of the villages nearby. Afterwards we will move back to Hoa Ban Homestay and summarise the shared experience. The length of the trip is about 9 hours.
Tour includes:
1. Fuel and transportation.
2. Breakfast, lunch and local snacks.
3. Local guide and staff expenses.
