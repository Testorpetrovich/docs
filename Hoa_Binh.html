Loop tour to Hoa Binh lake & Go Lao waterfall

4 hours
Previous Next × Close
Our adventure will begin with a climb to the spectacular observation point of Mai Chau (the Mai chau view poit). We will stop there to capture the contrast of the flat rice paddies and towering mountains in our memory and cameras. We will continue driving through the mountains of Thung Ke Pass and stop for a break with tea or coffe and local snacks at another view point. While your body will relax on bamboo floor at local style cafe your eyes explore the valley below and boundless mountain expanse. After that we'll go down to the valley we've just seen.The narrow road passes through the picturesque villages and corn plantations. Buffalo families will greet us with their lazily chewing faces. These cozy landscapes will suddenly give way to a breathtaking view of Hoa Binh Lake. This vast lake overflowed in the middle of the mountains and has a depth of 150 meters. Try to imagine this depth when you look at the special color of the surface of the lake. The Mường and Thai people who live on the lakeside are known for their fishing skills and we can see fishponds float between islands.Toward the end of the trip, we will take a refreshing dip in the Go Lao waterfall. For Jacuzzi lovers powerful jets of the waterfall will make a massage.After all that fun and experience we will go back to the starting point and that's how our loop finishes. Tour is about 4 hours long.
Tour includes:
1. Fuel and transportation.
2. Tea, coffee and local snacks.
3. Local guide and staff expenses.
